Our Vision

W.E.E. Serve Youth is a not-for-profit organization dedicated to empowering Black youth through mentorship, community-building, and exposure to opportunities that foster personal and professional growth. Our initiatives include sports that promote teamwork, discipline, and leadership while creating a sense of brotherhood among participants. Through one-on-one mentorship, we guide young people to navigate challenges, set goals, and build confidence in their abilities. In addition, we provide access to a wide range of experiences and ideas that may not be readily available to them, helping to unlock their potential and broaden their horizons. At W.E.E., we are committed to nurturing the next generation of leaders and creating lasting positive change in our community.

Our mission is to empower, uplift, and support Black and marginalized youth from low-income households and underserved communities. We strive to foster a resilient community that provides these young people equitable access to quality education, mentorship, mental health support, and career development opportunities. By addressing systemic barriers and promoting inclusivity, we aim to inspire a new generation of leaders, creators, and advocates who are equipped to thrive and drive positive change within their communities.

We envision a future where all young people, regardless of socioeconomic background, race, or neighbourhood, have the opportunity to thrive and realize their full potential. We aim to create an inclusive society where equity and justice are foundational, where Black and marginalized youth are uplifted and celebrated, and where their voices lead the way in shaping a better world.
